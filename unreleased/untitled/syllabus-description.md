Lebesque Integral 



$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \mathrm{d} \mu=\sum_{i=1}^{n} y_{i} \cdot \mu\left(A_{y_{i}}\right)
$$

and 



$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) d \mu=\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} \int_{a}^{b} f_{n}(x) d \mu
$$
